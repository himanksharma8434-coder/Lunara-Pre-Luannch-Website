4. Description of Service

Lunara is a menstrual cycle tracking and wellness companion application. The Service includes, but is not limited to, the following features:

  • Cycle Tracking: Log period start/end dates, predict future periods, ovulation windows, and fertile windows using adaptive statistical algorithms.
  • Symptom & Mood Logging: Track daily symptoms, moods, sleep, water intake, basal body temperature, and other wellness metrics.
  • AI Health Chat: Ask cycle-related and wellness questions powered by third-party AI/LLM services (Groq, Google Gemini). See Section 9 for important disclaimers.
  • Insights & Charts: Visualize trends in mood, sleep, steps, symptoms, and cycle patterns over time.
  • Health Platform Sync: Optionally sync steps, sleep, heart rate, weight, height, and menstrual data with Google Health Connect (Android) or Apple HealthKit (iOS).
  • Nearby Doctors: Discover healthcare facilities near your GPS location using OpenStreetMap data.
  • Partner Sync: Share your cycle data with a linked partner in real time (initiated by you).
  • Community Feed: Post, comment, and engage with other Lunara users.
  • PDF Health Reports: Export your health data summary as a PDF for your doctor.
  • Smart Notifications: Locally scheduled reminders for period predictions, ovulation windows, and daily wellness tips.
  • Cloud Sync: Securely back up your data to the cloud and restore it across devices.

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Lunara is NOT a medical device. Lunara is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or contraception.

  • Cycle predictions, fertile window estimates, and ovulation dates are statistical estimates based on your logged data and should not be relied upon for family planning or contraceptive purposes.
  • AI-generated health guidance is informational only and may be inaccurate (see Section 9).
  • Wellness plans, symptom analysis, and insights are for educational purposes only.
  • Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding any medical condition, treatment plan, or health concern.
  • Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of information provided by Lunara.
  • If you experience severe pain, abnormal symptoms, or any medical emergency, seek immediate medical attention.

9. AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

⚠️ IMPORTANT: AI/LLM Usage Disclosure

Lunara uses third-party Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Large Language Model (LLM) services to power the following features:

  • AI Health Chat — Powered by Groq (models: Qwen 3.6 27B, Mixtral 8x7B) and/or Google Gemini
  • AI Support Auto-Replies — Powered by Groq
  • AI Wellness Plan Suggestions — Powered by Groq / Gemini

9.1 How AI Works in Lunara

When you use the AI Chat or receive AI-generated content:

  1. Your current cycle phase, logged symptoms, mood, and recent health observations are combined into a text prompt.
  2. This prompt is sent to the third-party AI provider's API (Groq at api.groq.com or Google Gemini).
  3. The AI model generates a response, which is displayed to you in the App.

9.2 Accuracy & Reliability Limitations

  • AI responses are NOT medical advice. They are generated by machine learning models and may contain inaccuracies, hallucinations, or outdated information.
  • AI models may generate responses that sound authoritative but are factually incorrect.
  • AI responses are generic guidance and do not account for your complete medical history, medications, allergies, or individual health circumstances.
  • We do not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of any AI-generated content.

9.3 Data Handling

  • Your cycle context and questions are sent to Groq and/or Google for processing. See our Privacy Policy, Section 5 for details.
  • We do not send your name, email, or account credentials to AI providers.
  • Third-party AI providers may process and temporarily retain the prompts sent to them in accordance with their own privacy policies.

9.4 Your Responsibility

  • Do not rely on AI responses for medical decisions. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
  • Do not share highly sensitive personal information (such as full names, addresses, or medical record numbers) in AI chat messages.
